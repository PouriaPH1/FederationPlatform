find process with port 

netstat -ano | findstr LISTENING | findstr ":port"

ex:
netstat -ano | findstr LISTENING | findstr ":5011"



see info of task with pid:

tasklist /FI "PID eq your pid" /FO LIST


ex:
tasklist /FI "PID eq 19460" /FO LIST


kill task with pid :

taskkill /PID pid /F


ex:
taskkill /PID 19460 /F
